Our ADA for Warriors pool donates 25% of the pool operator rewards to 500 Rising. Donations are typically made during the last epoch of each month that our pool was active.

500 Rising was chosen because they are taking a stand on violence against women. Their first mission is to train female instructors in essential self-defense. They support these women as they reach out to their communities and networks making a difference in the stubborn statistics of violence against women.



To learn more about their mission, please visit their website.
