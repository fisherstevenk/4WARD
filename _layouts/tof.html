Our ADA for Warriors pool donates 30% of the pool operator rewards to the Third Option Foundation. Donations are typically made during the last epoch of each month that our pool was active.

The Third Option Foundation was chosen because they provide support for our fallen and injured CIA operators, and their families.

It was founded to fill the gap created by the fact that these operators function in a covert manner. Their covert nature leaves them ineligible for most charitable assistance available to US military members and their families. The assistance given can be in the form of financial and logistical support, academic and college prep for children, home maintenance, counseling and family services, physical health and wellness support, legacy activities to honor fallen warriors, and the establishment of memorials.

To learn more about their mission, please visit their website.
