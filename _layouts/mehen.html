Steve Fisher, our pool operator, is the CTO for Mehen.

Mehen is the brain child of Matt Plomin. The Mehen project will bring the first one to one, fiat backed stablecoin to the Cardano ecosystem called USDM.

The Mehen Project aims to achieve the following three key objectives.

  • (i) Issuance of a Cardano native stablecoin pegged 1-to-1 with the US dollar, backed fully and transparently by liquid US dollar-denominated assets.
  • (ii) Reinvestment of proceeds generated by the stablecoin protocol to fund further development on the Cardano blockchain.
  • (iii) Improving decentralized ownership and governance of both efforts via a Mehen tokenholdersâ€™ decentralized autonomous organization (DAO).
